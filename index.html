Welcome to Goob Horizon

Get ready to experience the most chaotic, high-octane, and downright silly open-world racing festival ever created. Goob Horizon isn't about prestige or perfectly tuned supercars; it's about speed, physics-defying stunts, and the pure joy of driving absolutely ridiculous vehicles.

The World: The Goob Archipelago

Instead of a single country, you are let loose on a vibrant, shifting chain of islands connected by giant, floating highways and off-road dirt paths.

  • The Glitch Zone: A neon-soaked city where the roads literally rearrange themselves while you are driving.
  • Mount Wobble: A massive, jagged peak with treacherous, winding roads that demand precise drifting (or just plain luck).
  • The Slippy Wetlands: A mud-drenched swamp where traction is a myth and "skidding" is the primary mode of travel.

The Vehicle Roster

In Goob Horizon, if it has wheels (or something resembling them), you can drive it.

Vehicle Class Description Notable Examples
Speedy Goobs Lightweight, twitchy, and incredibly fast. Modified lawnmowers, Go-karts, Rocket-skates.
Heavy Bonkers Massive, indestructible, and impossible to steer. Armored School Buses, Steamrollers, Monster Trucks.
Wacky Specials Just plain weird vehicles. Giant Shopping Carts, Inflatable Beach Balls (with thrusters).

Gameplay Features

  • "Oops" Physics Engine: We’ve tuned the physics to be intentionally bouncy and forgiving. You won't crash; you’ll just perform a spectacular, multi-flip tumble and keep going.
  • The Goob-o-Meter: The more chaotic and reckless you drive, the faster your "Goob-o-Meter" fills. Once full, you trigger "Turbo Mode," where your vehicle turns into a neon-glowing projectile.
  • Customizable Chaos: Use the livery editor to cover your vehicle in googly eyes, sticky notes, or animated patterns.
  • Horizon Arcade (Goob Edition): Collaborative mini-games scattered across the map, like "King of the Hill" played with bumper cars or "Precision Parking" where you have to land on a moving blimp.

Your Festival, Your Rules

You start as a nobody in a beat-up go-kart and work your way up to becoming the Grand Goob of the Horizon. Along the way, you collect "Smiles" (our version of experience points) by winning races, discovering secret shortcuts, and completing dangerous stunts.

Pro Tip: Don't worry about hitting the guardrails. In Goob Horizon, they're usually made of rubber and will just launch you back onto the track with a satisfying boing sound!
